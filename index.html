About me

A small introduction about myself

Born and raised in Istanbul. Currently based in Los Angeles. Studying at USC with a BA in Communication, and minors in Media Arts + Practice, and Music Industry.

I am an artist with many different mediums, ranging from graphic design to music production, drawing & painting to videography. After beginning my studies at USC, my creative passion for artistic expression has merged with an extensive interest in the entertainment industry - music, film, television, technology, and fashion.

Combining the viewpoint of an optimistic artist and the mindset of a global citizen, I am bringing a novel, entrepreneurial, and divergent perspective to my work experiences and environments. Always having a detail-oriented, socially energetic and open-minded approach has helped me excel in both academic and work environments, and becoming an exceptional leader both dependently and interdependently while multitasking.
